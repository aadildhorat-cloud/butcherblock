About Us

Established in 2018, Butcher Block is a family business run on family values. We supply premium meat to clients across South Africa with the highest quality products from our own production and trusted partners. Relentless about quality, uncompromising about food safety, and passionate about service – we strive to build lasting relationships and add value to our customers.
